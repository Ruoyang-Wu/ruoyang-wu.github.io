I am a sophomore in Computer Science at [Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU)](https://www.nwpu.edu.cn/), Xi'an, China (GPA: 3.95/4.1, Rank: **1/193**). My research interests lie in **Multimodal Large Language Models**, **Computer Vision**, and **Embodied AI**.

## Research

I am working on multi-modal aerial-ground cross-view place recognition under the supervision of Prof. Jiaqi Yang at the School of Computer Science, NWPU. My work focuses on addressing viewpoint discrepancy and distribution shift between ground sensors and aerial maps, through a physical prior-guided Mixture-of-Experts model and information bottleneck regularization.

I also work at the NWPU Soccer Robot Base, developing motion control and computer vision algorithms for NAO humanoid robots.
