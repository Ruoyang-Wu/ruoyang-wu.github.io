We propose a multi-modal fusion framework for aerial-ground cross-view place recognition, addressing the challenging viewpoint discrepancy and distribution shift between ground sensors and aerial maps.

**Key contributions:**
- **Physical Prior-Guided MoE**: extracts physical statistics from raw inputs as routing conditions, enabling adaptive expert selection for varying environmental conditions
- **Information Bottleneck Regularization**: models cross-view alignment as an information distillation process, filtering ground-view-specific noise while preserving essential information shared with the aerial view
